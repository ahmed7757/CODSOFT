Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, one of the greatest football players of all time.

About the Legend

☛ Cristiano Ronaldo was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal.

☛ He is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Al-Nassr and captains the Portugal national team.

☛ Ronaldo has won numerous awards, including five Ballon d'Or titles, and is regarded as one of the best players in football history.

☛ He began his senior club career playing for Sporting CP before signing with Manchester United in 2003, where he won his first Ballon d'Or at age 23.

☛ In 2009, he transferred to Real Madrid in a record-breaking transfer, where he became the club's all-time top scorer and won four more Ballon d'Or titles.

☛ Ronaldo later played for Juventus and then returned to Manchester United before joining Al-Nassr in 2023.

☛ He is the all-time top scorer in the UEFA Champions League and holds the record for the most goals scored in a single season in the competition.

☛ With Portugal, Ronaldo has won the UEFA European Championship in 2016 and the UEFA Nations League in 2019.

☛ Known for his speed, dribbling, precise finishing, and leadership on the field, Ronaldo has broken multiple records throughout his career.

☛ In addition to his football career, Ronaldo is a global ambassador for various charities and brands, making significant contributions off the field.
